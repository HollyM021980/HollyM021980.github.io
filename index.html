MovnThere

MovnThere facilitates discovery and capture of “favorites so far” as people research where they will live during their next adventure!! This can be done today but it requires searching multiple places and a separate mechanism for storing research results along the way. MovnThere provides a one-stop search site for communities and see info such as census data, transportation and local points of interest (arts/museums, parks etc) and store favorites along the way!
